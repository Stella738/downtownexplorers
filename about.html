About Us

The Downtown Explorers Club was created by Sheridan students who wanted to make exploring Toronto more accessible and enjoyable for everyone. We organize casual outings to different parts of the city, from cozy cafes and art galleries to scenic parks and local events.

Our Goal

Our goal is to help students build friendships, feel confident navigating downtown, and experience the diverse culture Toronto has to offer. Whether you've just moved here or you've been in the area for a while, you're always welcome to join and share new adventures together.
